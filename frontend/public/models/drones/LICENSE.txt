Bundled 3D Drone Models — License Information

All bundled drone models in this directory are procedurally generated
placeholder meshes created by the TarmacView development team.

These models are released under the CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain
Dedication) license. You may use them freely without attribution.

Models:
- generic_quadcopter.glb — generic four-rotor drone placeholder
- dji_matrice_300.glb — DJI Matrice 300 RTK style placeholder
- dji_mavic_3.glb — DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise style placeholder
- generic_hexacopter.glb — six-rotor heavy-lift drone placeholder
- generic_fixed_wing.glb — fixed-wing VTOL survey drone placeholder

Thumbnails in thumbnails/ are rendered from the above models.

Custom-uploaded models (stored in backend/static/models/custom/) remain
the property of the uploading user and are not covered by this license.
